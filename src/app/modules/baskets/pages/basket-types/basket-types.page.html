By investing through Reef, you can choose to invest into multiple types of basket, whether you want to take a higher risk, or just have a steady yield -- it's up to you. Below are the basket types we currently support.
Volatile Basket
This basket comprises of Uniswap, Balancer and Mooniswap pools as well as ERC-20 coins, which are selected as the highest yielding baskets based on our algorithm. You can use our pre-selected pools and ETH allocation in them, or you can customize it, using the pools/coins you want and choose in which pool or coin you want to invest the most ETH. You can also tell our algorithm the level of Risk you want to take. This will make the return higher, but might make the loss higher as well.
Stablecoin Basket
The Stablecoin Basket is a basket comprised of Stablecoins from different protocols which our algorithm selects for you based on the highest APY (Annual Percentage Yield). It includes Coins from protocols such as Dfi, Yearn, Curve, and more. You can choose the amount of ETH you wish to invest in each Stablecoin, and you can choose to invest a minimum of three and maximum of ten Stablecoins. They will always be selected starting from the highest APY, to the lowest. You can also view the ROI (Return of Investment) of every combination of coins you make.
